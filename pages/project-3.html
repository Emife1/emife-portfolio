Recovery Breakdown

Duplicate Payments Identified

$18.2K

Used Excel VBA and advanced formulas to flag transactions with matching vendors, amounts, and dates within 30-day windows.

Pricing Error Recovery

$11.5K

Identified overcharges by comparing invoiced rates against contracts and vendor agreements using detailed reconciliation.

Controls Implemented

Duplicate Prevention

Automated system checks for duplicate vendors/amounts. Manual override with supervisor approval required.

Vendor Verification

Enhanced verification checklist. New vendors require vendor insurance, W-9, and business registration verification.

Fraud Scorecard

Quarterly fraud risk assessment. Monitors unusual patterns, vendor concentration, and exception transactions.
